BSSNet is a real-time semantic segmentation network for road scenes inspired by autoencoder structures. The project focuses on the trade-off among segmentation accuracy, inference speed, and model complexity for practical road-scene understanding.

The related paper, "BSSNet: A Real-Time Semantic Segmentation Network for Road Scenes Inspired from AutoEncoder", was published in IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology.
